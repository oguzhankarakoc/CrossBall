FAQ

  • Do I need an account? No. CrossBall uses an anonymous ID on your device.
  • When does the daily puzzle refresh? Every day at 00:00 UTC.
  • How do I restore Premium? Open Settings → Restore Purchases (same Apple ID).
  • Hints show one player — can I answer another? Yes. Hints describe one possible answer; any valid intersection player counts.
  • Delete my data? Email us with your in-app user UUID (Settings) or device details.
