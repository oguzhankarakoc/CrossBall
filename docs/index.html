CrossBall

Connect clubs. Prove your football IQ.

How it works

  • 3×3 grid — row and column clubs define each cell
  • Find the link — name a player who played for both clubs
  • Daily puzzle — one global challenge every day, build your streak
  • Rare picks score higher — obscure answers beat obvious names

Features

  • Smart hints when you're stuck
  • Weekly leaderboard & level progression
  • Practice mode, friend challenges & community goals
  • Optional Premium — unlimited practice, no ads
